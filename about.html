About SEA College

The South East Asian Education Trust was established in 2000 with the mission to provide academic excellence to all sections of society. SEA College of Engineering and Technology (SEACET), founded in 2007 by Sri A. Krishnappa, is committed to blending modern education with traditional Indian values.

Our 50-acre campus in Bangalore offers world-class infrastructure, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development. We aim to empower students with the skills and values needed to excel in a global landscape.

Vision

To be a global leader in engineering education, fostering innovation and excellence.

Mission

To provide quality education, disciplined personality development, and research opportunities.
